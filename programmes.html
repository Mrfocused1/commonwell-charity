Our Programmes

Six free programmes, open to all — no referral, no means test, no catch. Whatever you need, the gate is open.

Our programmes

Six ways we help a neighbourhood thrive

Each programme is free, open to all, and shaped by the people who use it. Here's what's growing right now.

Green spaces

Community Gardens

Open to all ages · 31 sites · No experience needed

We turn neglected land into shared gardens where neighbours grow food, friendships and fresh air together.

How it works

  1. Drop in to your nearest garden — no booking, just turn up.
  2. A volunteer host hands you tools and shows you the ropes.
  3. Take home what you grow, and help shape the next planting.
Youth & play

Youth & Play Clubs

Ages 5–16 · Term-time & holidays · Free hot meal

Safe, free after-school and holiday clubs that keep young people active, curious and supported.

How it works

  1. Register your child once — it takes about five minutes.
  2. They join sport, crafts and trips with trained, DBS-checked staff.
  3. Every session ends with a free hot meal before home time.
Skills & training

Skills & Training

Adults 16+ · Weekly · CV, digital & trade skills

Free courses and mentoring that help people back into work, learning or confidence — at any age.

How it works

  1. Tell us your goal — a job, a skill or just a fresh start.
  2. We match you to a free course and a personal mentor.
  3. Leave with a certificate, a CV and someone in your corner.
Active together

Active Together

All abilities · Year-round · Just turn up

Free park runs, walking groups and team games that get the whole neighbourhood moving and connected.

How it works

  1. Pick a session — run, walk or play, whatever suits you.
  2. Show up in comfy shoes; no kit and no fitness needed.
  3. Stick around for a cuppa and a chat afterwards.
Mind & body

Mind & Body

Adults · Weekly · No referral required

Drop-in wellbeing sessions — movement, breathing and a friendly ear — for anyone feeling the weight of it all.

How it works

  1. Come as you are — no referral, no forms, no cost.
  2. Move, breathe and unwind in a gentle guided session.
  3. Talk to a trained listener whenever you need one.
Connection

Connected Elders

Ages 65+ · Weekly visits · Free transport

Befriending visits, shared lunches and lifts to appointments that turn isolated days into looked-after ones.

How it works

  1. Sign up — for yourself or someone you're worried about.
  2. We match a friendly, background-checked companion.
  3. Enjoy weekly visits, lunches and lifts to appointments.

The common thread

What every programme shares

However different they look, all six are built on the same three promises.

Free, always

Every session is free at the point of need — funded by donations, partners and grants, so cost is never the reason anyone misses out.

Open to everyone

No referral, no means test, no experience required. Whoever you are and whatever your week looks like, the gate is open.

Led by local people

Our programmes are run with the neighbourhood, not just for it — shaped, hosted and looked after by the people who use them.

Find the programme for you

Whether you want to take part, lend a hand or help keep a programme running, there's a place here with your name on it.
