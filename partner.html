A day in the garden with us is a day that stays with people. Bring your class or your colleagues, and watch a neighbourhood grow because of it.

For schools

Outdoor learning days

Half- and full-day programmes that take the classroom into the garden — growing, cooking, building and looking after a living space. Curriculum-linked and fully supervised.

  • Half day, full day or termly
  • All abilities and SEND welcome
  • Free for schools in our priority areas

Team volunteering days

Swap the away-day for a day that matters. Your team spends it building, planting and decorating alongside the people we serve — and your support keeps a programme running all year.

  • Teams of 6–40
  • Real, hands-on impact in a day
  • Corporate partnership packages

There's a fit for everyone

More ways to partner

Big or small, one-off or year-round — every kind of support keeps a garden open and a club running.

Corporate volunteering days

Roll up your sleeves together. Spend a day building raised beds, painting the hall or clearing a new plot — practical work with a real, visible impact by home time.

Payroll giving

Let your team give a little each month straight from their pay, tax-free. It's simple to set up, costs you nothing, and quietly funds programmes all year round.

Sponsor a programme

Put your name behind a garden, a youth club or an elders' lunch for a year. We'll keep you close to the impact, with updates and visits from the people you help.

Gifts in kind

Tools, timber, plants, printing, professional time — if you've got it to spare, chances are we can put it to good use. Tell us what you have, and we'll find it a home.
