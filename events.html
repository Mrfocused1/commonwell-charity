Host an event

Celebrate with us — and give back

Birthday, anniversary, christening, work do or street party — bring it to one of our community gardens and your celebration becomes something bigger. We'll look after the space; you bring the people, the cake and the playlist. Every penny of your donation stays right here, funding the clubs and gardens on your doorstep.

  • The space — a garden, covered terrace or community hall, yours for the afternoon.
  • The setup — tables, chairs, a fridge and a covered area if the weather turns British.
  • The cause — your suggested donation funds a child's place at our holiday club.
  • The help — a friendly volunteer host on hand from the first balloon to the last cup.

What's included

One simple suggested donation covers everything you need for a lovely afternoon.

A space that fits

Choose the garden, the covered terrace or the community hall — whichever suits your crowd, your celebration and the great British weather.

Everything set up

Tables, chairs, a fridge for the fizz and a sheltered corner if the clouds roll in. You arrive to a space that's ready and waiting.

A volunteer host

A friendly face from the neighbourhood is on hand throughout — to point out the kettle, lend a hand and make sure the day runs sweetly.

From idea to party

How it works

Three easy steps between you and a day that matters.

01

Pick a date & space

Tell us when you're celebrating and which garden you fancy. We'll check the diary and hold the spot for you.

02

Set your suggested donation

Choose what you'd like to give — from £80 upwards. Every pound funds a programme right in your neighbourhood.

03

Turn up and enjoy

Bring the people you love to a space that's already set up. Your volunteer host takes care of the rest.

Make it a day that matters

Celebrate the moments that count, in a place that gives back. Whether you host with us, give or roll up your sleeves to help, you keep your neighbourhood growing.
