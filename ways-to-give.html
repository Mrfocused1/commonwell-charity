Other ways to give

There's more than one way to make a difference. Pick whatever fits your life.

Gift Aid

If you're a UK taxpayer, ticking Gift Aid adds 25% to every donation at no extra cost to you — turning your £10 into £12.50 for the neighbourhood.

Fundraise for us

Run, bake, walk or shave it off — set up a fundraising page for your big idea and rally your friends behind a programme close to home.

Payroll giving

Give straight from your pay before tax, so a little more reaches us for less. Ask your employer about Give As You Earn and we'll do the rest.

A gift in your will

Once your loved ones are looked after, a gift in your will keeps gardens growing and neighbours connected for the generations who follow.

Where your money goes

Every pound, put to work

We're a small charity that's careful with what you give and open about where it goes. Your gift doesn't disappear into overheads — it lands in a garden, a club or a kitchen near someone who needs it.

  • 88p in every £1 goes straight to programmes
  • Independently examined accounts
  • Full breakdown in our annual report
